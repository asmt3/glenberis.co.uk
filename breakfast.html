Breakfast

We use locally sourced ingredients, with lots of choice. Breakfast Buffet includes choice of cereals, fruit, yoghurts, smoothies, juices and baked goods followed by a traditional Norfolk cooked breakfast or a lighter option if you prefer. We also offer many other dishes including Eggs Benedict, Florentine or Royale, Smoked Haddock and poached egg, Smoked salmon and creamy scrambled eggs, Sundancer Eggs and many more and if it isn't on the menu please ask and we will cook it for you!
