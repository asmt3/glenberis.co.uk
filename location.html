Location

Hunstanton, affectionately known as Sunny Hunny, is a traditional British Seaside town with many local amenities, including shops, theatre, amusements, small funfair, wonderful beach and unique red and white stripped cliffs. It is the only resort on the east Coast which faces WEST so can enjoy brilliant sunsets over the sea. Sporting and leisure facilities

The beautiful North Norfolk coast and countryside , designated as an area of outstanding natural beauty are on our doorstep ready to explore.

Glenberis is ideal location for a relaxing break and an escape from everyday living. You can look forward to an exceptional welcome from Paul and Barbara who have just taken over ownership of Glenberis, having spent two very successful years at Rose-Fitt House also in Hunstanton.
